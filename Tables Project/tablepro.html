Our Services



  • HP Warranty and Support Services:-
    HP provides comprehensive warranty and support services for
    its Victus gaming laptops. This includes a standard one-year
    limited hardware warranty with 24/7 technical assistance,
    extended warranty options, and accidental damage protection plans.


  • HP Omen Gaming Hub:-
    The HP Omen Gaming Hub is a pre-installed software suite designed
    to enhance the gaming experience on HP Victus laptops. It includes
    tools for system optimization, overclocking, performance monitoring,
    and network boost to reduce latency.

  • HP Cloud Recovery Tool:-
    The HP Cloud Recovery Tool allows users to create a recovery drive
    or perform a system reset by downloading the necessary software and
    drivers directly from the HP cloud. This service ensures that if the
    system encounters serious issues, users can easily restore their HP
    Victus laptop to its original factory settings, minimizing downtime
    and data loss.

  • HP SmartFriend:-
    HP SmartFriend is a subscription-based service offering personalized
    technical support for HP devices, including Victus gaming laptops.
    Subscribers get access to expert assistance for a variety of tech issues,
    from software installation and setup to troubleshooting hardware
    problems.
